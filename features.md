# Features

## Academic
- Assign modules to lecturers and manage teaching loads.
- Maintain module catalogues and semester offerings.
- Capture assessments, enter marks, and review gradebooks.
- Record class attendance and view attendance summaries.
- Generate course summaries and attendance reports.

## Admissions
- Define intake periods, certificate types, subjects, and entry requirements.
- Track applicants and their submitted applications, including pending counts.

## Registry
- Keep student records up to date and manage enrollment status.
- Handle registration requests, clearances, and related documents.
- Process graduation requests, schedules, and certificate issuance.
- Manage blocked students and academic term definitions.
- Provide reports for enrollments, graduations, and board of examination.

## Finance
- Review and decide on registration clearance requests.
- Review and decide on graduation clearance requests.
- Track sponsored students and manage sponsor relationships.
- Record payment receipts and related finance reports.
- Provide clearance status views for finance and supporting departments.

## Admin
- Track tasks across teams with notification counts for pending work.
- Manage user accounts, roles, and system notifications.
- Maintain shared reference data such as modules, semester modules, schools, and sponsors.
- Provide tools like simulators, grade calculators, and grade lookup.
- Run bulk exports such as transcript batches and view admin reports.

## LMS
- Manage courses, outlines, and enrolled students.
- Share learning materials, posts, assignments, and quizzes.
- Record grades within the LMS gradebook.
- Support virtual classroom sessions.

## Timetable
- Publish timetable views for classes.
- Manage timetable allocations and time slots.
- Maintain venue listings and venue types.

## Library
- Catalogue books with authors, categories, and copies.
- Track loans, due items, and fines.
- List external libraries and other learning resources.

## Audit Logs
- Record changes to students, programs, semesters, and module enrollments for traceability.

## Dashboard
- Provides the landing experience and navigation into each module.

## Auth
- Supports user login and initial account setup.

## Student Portal
- Student-facing home with profile information.
- Self-service registration steps and Fortinet account registration.
- View transcripts and graduation progress.
